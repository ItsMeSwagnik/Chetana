Understanding Depression

What is Depression?

Depression is more than just feeling sad or going through a rough patch. It's a serious mental health condition that affects how you feel, think, and handle daily activities.

Common Signs & Symptoms

  • Persistent sad, anxious, or "empty" mood
  • Loss of interest in activities once enjoyed
  • Fatigue and decreased energy
  • Difficulty concentrating or making decisions
  • Changes in sleep patterns
  • Changes in appetite or weight

Coping Strategies

🌅 Daily Routine

Maintain a consistent daily schedule to provide structure and stability.

🏃‍♀️ Physical Activity

Regular exercise can boost mood and energy levels naturally.

🤝 Social Connection

Stay connected with friends and family, even when you don't feel like it.

🎯 Small Goals

Set achievable daily goals to build momentum and confidence.

When to Seek Help

If symptoms persist for more than two weeks or interfere with daily life, consider reaching out to a mental health professional.

Behavioral Activation

What is Behavioral Activation?

Behavioral Activation is a therapeutic approach that focuses on scheduling positive activities to improve mood and break the cycle of depression.

How It Works

When we're depressed, we often withdraw from activities we once enjoyed. This creates a negative cycle where inactivity leads to worse mood, which leads to more inactivity.

Breathing Exercise

4-7-8 Breathing Technique

This technique helps activate your body's relaxation response and reduce anxiety.

Ready to begin?

How to Practice:

  1. Inhale through your nose for 4 counts
  2. Hold your breath for 7 counts
  3. Exhale through your mouth for 8 counts
  4. Repeat 4-8 cycles

Benefits:

  • Reduces anxiety and stress
  • Improves sleep quality
  • Lowers heart rate
  • Promotes relaxation

Privacy & Policy

🔒 Your Privacy Matters

DPDP Act 2023 Compliant

चेtanā is committed to protecting your personal health information in compliance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP) 2023.

📊 What Data We Collect

  • Assessment Scores: PHQ-9, GAD-7, and PSS-10 responses and calculated scores
  • Mood Logs: Daily mood ratings and journal entries
  • Account Information: Email address, name, and date of birth (if registered)
  • Usage Data: App interaction patterns for improving user experience

🌍 Where Your Data is Stored

All data is securely stored in encrypted databases within India (Mumbai region) to comply with data localization requirements under DPDP Act.

👥 Who Can Access Your Data

  • You: Full access to view, export, and delete your data
  • Licensed Clinicians: Only with your explicit consent for treatment purposes
  • Admin: Anonymized data for system maintenance and improvement
  • No Third Parties: We never sell or share your personal health data

⚙️ Your Rights Under DPDP Act

📝 Right to Information

Know what data we collect and how it's used

📊 Right to Access

View and export all your stored data

✏️ Right to Correction

Update or correct your personal information

🗑️ Right to Erasure

Delete your account and all associated data

🗓️ Data Retention Policy

  • Active Accounts: Data retained as long as account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Automatically deleted after 12 months of inactivity
  • Deleted Accounts: All data permanently removed within 30 days
  • Backup Data: Removed from backups within 90 days

🔐 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Multi-factor authentication for admin access

📧 Contact Us

For any privacy concerns or to exercise your rights:

  • Response Time: Within 72 hours as per DPDP Act
